STEM COURSES

Cab Calloway School does not just specialize in the arts. In fact, it has some of the strongest STEM programs in the state of Delaware. Students are required to take a set amount of science credits, and then are allowed to extend their knowledge though AP classes. Cab students can also take classes at Charter School of Wilmington, which shares a building with Cab. Charter offers many more advanced science classes, such as Astronomy or Nanotechnology.

The core component of Cab Calloway's STEM career pathway is its Digital Media program. Digital Media combines the arts and techology to create a fully integrated program, which helps students get a head start in life. Digital Media encompasses a variety of subjects, from photography to web design, and from graphic design to cinematography.

WILLIAM GREIDER
HEAD OF DIGITAL MEDIA

Digital Media Majors encompass a wide variety of skills, everything from: creative writing, capturing images, designing logos, making layouts in print media, designing websites, and making cinematic works of art.

LEARN MORE

CURRICULUM

DIGITAL MEDIA 1

PRINT DESIGN + PHOTOGRAPHY

The first part of the Digital Media curriculum starts in the students' first year with Digital Media 1. Digital Media 1 starts off by helping you get used to using a computer to get stuff done. It then goes into print design, and the beginnings of photography.

SYLLABUS

DIGITAL MEDIA 2

PRINT + WEB DESIGN

Digital Media 2 continues the education on print media, moving into magazine designs. After print design is finished, the curriculum then goes into web design, teaching the basics of HTML and CSS. By the end of the year, students are able to code a complete website.

SYLLABUS

DIGITAL MEDIA 3

PHOTOGRAPHY + CINEMA

Digital Media 3 changes gears, returning to photography, and going more in-depth into the mechanics of the camera. After the photography section, students start to learn cinematography. The final project of the class is a 2-3 minute video.

SYLLABUS

DIGITAL MEDIA 4

INDEPENDENT STUDY

In Digital Media 4, students are able to choose their own project, working independently. They create their own rubrics and timeline, and then work over the year to complete that project. Students can choose not to take this class if they want to pursue other subjects.

SCIENCE OLYMPIAD

Cab Calloway's Science Olympiad team is one of the top-ranking teams in the state. The club has around thirty people, who meet a few times a week throughout the year and work together in order to participate in the events at the state competition.

MATH LEAGUE

The Cab Calloway Math League team consists of about twenty people: fifteen upperclassmen and five freshmen. They meet around once a week throughout the year, and participate in four meets, generally sending many people to states.

ODYSSEY OF THE MIND

Cab Calloway has had a lot of success over the past few years with Odyssey of the Mind. Many teams have ended up going to the world competition. These teams are formed by individuals, and then consult with a few teachers in order to get registered.

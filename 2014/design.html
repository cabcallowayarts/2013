Now that you have learned how to code a website, you have to know what you are going to code. This brings us to the concept of design. This is the process of bringing an idea from your head into reality. Web design is its own form of expression, with its own methods to reach this expression, and this is what we will be teaching you.

At this point, the in-site compiler might not be enough to support the code that you will be writing. At this point, it might be smart to actually move over to a text-editor, such as Notepad or TextEdit. If you forgethow this works, you can go back to the introduction on the home page for a step-by-step walkthrough of how to do this. To get started, choose "The First Steps in Design".

What Not to Do

  • Overpaying

    • Don't overpay for services - there are reliable websites that charge extremely low amounts, especially if your website is small.

  • Capitalization

    • Don't add capitalizations or spaces in your file names
      GOOD: "page_1.html" BAD: "Page 1.html"

  • Long Names

    • Don't choose a complicated or long domain name - internet users need something easy to remember and hard to mess up.

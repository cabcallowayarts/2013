Commercial applications of augmented reality effect a wide range people including the consumers who use AR products, companies looking to market their product through AR, and the practical applications of AR that assist companies in efficiency. Augmented reality is an invaluable technological development because of the many applications it has, maximizing uses and benefits with slight variation of technology and minimizing concerns.

ikea catalog

This application allows consumers to view how furniture would appear in their space by virtually placing the furniture into the space using augmented reality. A smartphone or tablet’s camera is used to recognize the product from the catalog then by placing the catalog in the place where the product is wanted, the consumer gets a three-dimensional perspective of how the item will work in the space. The consumer is then able to rotate, and reposition the items. This application allows consumers to make a more educated and confident decision about their purchases, overall benefiting the IKEA company.

wikitude drive

Navigation applications are possibly the most natural fit of augmented reality with our everyday lives. Enhanced GPS systems are using augmented reality to make it easier to get from point A to point B. Wikitude Drive for the Android operating system which is currently in beta brings the GPS into the 21st century. Using the phone's camera in combination with the GPS, the users see the selected route over the live view of what is in front of the car.

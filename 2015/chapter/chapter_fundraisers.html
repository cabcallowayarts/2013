FUNDRAISERS

DODGEBALL TOURNAMENT

CCSA TSA will be hosting its second, epic, tri-school dodgeball tournament. Over 40 teams will enter the arena. Only one will exit victorious. Our TSA chapter has been rounding up referees, contest judges, and a DJ to make this tournament one to remember. All funds will allow us to travel to Nationals in Dallas this June.

TEE SHIRTS

Harnessing the skills of our chapter's digital artists, we designed and printed over 200 T-shirts to raise funds for nationals. The varied designs included a promotional graphic for our school's 20th anniversary and a stylish abstract design to appeal to younger audiences. Our members worked their sales skills at shows, events, and school functions.

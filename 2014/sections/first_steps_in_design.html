First Steps in Design

Establish and Objective and Target Audience

You need to decide what you want your website to achieve. What is the purpose of your website? With your purpose and objective decided, you need to think of who is going to see your website and who you want to see your website. Deciding a target audience will help you in the design process with what images, texts, and other visuals you will have.

Start Designing

Now that we know who we want to see the website we can start designing. The first step in designing is getting all your ideas out of your head. Research different website and look at the layouts. See how different websites portray their content. Now we need to start drawing layouts and choose the best ones. Keep in mind that goal of a website design is to provide content in a way that is pleasing to the eye.

Eating is worse than cellphones

When one thinks of distracted driving, normally cellphones come to mind but in fact, 80% of crashes are caused by the driver eating or drinking while driving. This may seem implausible, that your breakfast on the go could cause a crash but it is very likely! Eating and drinking in the car causes the driver to have late reactions and sudden stops. Messy foods are even more dangerous.Simple snacks like chocolate, coffee and other foods like fried chicken, tacos and soups are infact very dangerous and distracting while behind the wheel.

Minor Snacks, Major Distractions

You wouldn't think that these minor snacks could cause life changing crashes but even a minor spill with your morning coffee could have a fatal outcome. It is a proven fact that eating distracts you just enough to cause delayed reactions to situations like sudden stops and increases the likeliness to get into head on collision. It is also said that in some cases, eating and driving can be even more dangerous than texting and driving! Just remember your food can wait, control your fate.

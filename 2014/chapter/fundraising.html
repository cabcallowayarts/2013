Fundraising

Dodgeball

For the first time ever, CCSA TSA will be hosting an epic, three-school dodgeball tournament. Over 40 teams will enter the arena. Only one will exit victorious. Our TSA chapter has been rounding up referees, contest judges, and a DJ to make this tournament one to remember. All funds will allow us to travel to Nationals in Washington, D.C. this June.

T-Shirts

Harnessing the skills of our chapter's digital artists, we designed and printed over 200 T-shirts to raise funds for nationals. The varied designs included a promotional graphic for our school's 20th anniversary and a stylish abstract design to appeal to younger audiences. Our members worked their sales skills at shows, events, and school functions.
